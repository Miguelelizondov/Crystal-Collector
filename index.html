Crystal Collectors!

This is the best game in the world

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of point to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total socre to the random number, you lose the game if your score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the starting value of the crystals.
